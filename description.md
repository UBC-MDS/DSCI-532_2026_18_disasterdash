DSCI 532 - Disaster Dash

Disaster Dash is a comprehensive dashboard which interactively visualizes global natural disasters.
User's may sort/filter data by country/region, disaster-type, economic fallout, casualties, and more.

This can be a useful tool for investigative research as well as emergency response preparedness.
Whether you are an independent researcher or a large organization like the UN, Disaster Dash can help!
