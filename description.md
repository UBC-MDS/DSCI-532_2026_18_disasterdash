DSCI 532 - Group 18 - Disaster Dash

Disaster Dash is a comprehensive dashboard tialored towards assisting emergency response and aid organizations with the task of assessing the cost of disasters economically as well as in human life. 
The dashboard can interactively visualize global natural disasters and enable users to sort and filter data by country/region, disaster-type, economic fallout, casualties, and more.

This can be powerful tool for investigative research as well as emergency response preparedness for private indivual research as well as distaster aid organizations of all sizes and scope.
